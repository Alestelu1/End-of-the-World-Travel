# End of the World Travel

An independent editorial publication documenting Southern Chile, Patagonia, the Magallanes and Chilean Antarctic Region, and the gateway to Antarctica.

---

## Mission

End of the World Travel is an editorial project dedicated to documenting the geography, history, culture, natural heritage, scientific importance and responsible exploration of Southern Chile.

Rather than functioning as a tourism marketplace, the publication seeks to become a trusted documentary reference built on verified information, official sources and respectful storytelling.

The project grows gradually, prioritizing quality, transparency and long-term editorial value.

---

## Vision

To become one of the most trusted independent editorial references for Southern Chile and the Chilean gateway to Antarctica.

The long-term vision includes documenting destinations, routes, protected areas, museums, maritime heritage, Indigenous cultures, scientific research and regional development while respecting local communities and natural environments.

---

## Editorial Principles

The publication is guided by several core principles:

* Independent editorial voice
* Documentary approach
* Geographic and historical accuracy
* Respect for local communities
* Respect for Indigenous peoples
* Respect for protected areas
* Responsible travel
* Transparency in sources
* Long-term maintainability

Editorial quality always takes priority over publication speed.

## Sources

Whenever possible, content is based on official and authoritative sources.

Priority includes:

* Government of Chile
* INE Chile
* INACH
* CONAF
* SERNATUR
* Armada de Chile
* Universities
* Museums
* UNESCO
* Scientific publications

International sources complement—but do not replace—official Chilean references.

---

## Photography

The publication uses only real photographs with appropriate licensing or permission.

AI-generated images may be used internally for design concepts and planning, but are not published as documentary representations of real places.

Every published photograph includes proper attribution.
