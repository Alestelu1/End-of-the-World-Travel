# End of the World Travel
## Design System & Editorial Principles
Version 1.0

---

# Project Vision

End of the World Travel is not a travel agency.

It is an independent editorial publication documenting the geography, history, culture, communities and natural heritage of Southern Chile.

Its objective is to document rather than promote.

The project seeks to present Chile's southern territories with respect, accuracy and visual quality while encouraging responsible travel.

---

# Editorial Principles

Always:

✓ respectful

# Sources Priority

Always prioritize Chilean official sources.

Order of trust:

1. Chilean Government
2. SERNATUR
3. CONAF
4. INACH
5. INE Chile
6. Armada de Chile
7. DGAC
8. UMAG
9. Museums
10. Ruta de los Parques
11. Local municipalities
12. Scientific publications

Wikipedia may only be used to locate primary references.

# Long-Term Vision

The current website is Version 1.

Its purpose is to establish a trustworthy editorial presence documenting Southern Chile.

Growth should be gradual.

New sections, destinations and features should only be added after editorial review.

The objective is not to become the largest tourism website, but one of the most reliable documentary references for Chile's southern territories.

Every future improvement should preserve:

• simplicity
• authenticity
• editorial independence
• respect for local communities
• respect for nature
• factual accuracy
