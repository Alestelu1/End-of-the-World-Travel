# Partners

**Project:** End of the World Travel

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Partnership & Collaboration Policy

---

# Purpose

This document defines how End of the World Travel approaches partnerships, collaborations, sponsorships, and affiliate relationships.

The publication exists primarily as an independent editorial project documenting Southern Chile.

Commercial activities should support the project without compromising editorial integrity.

---

# Editorial Independence

Editorial decisions always come first.

No partner may influence:

Historical interpretation

Geographic information

Scientific information

Editorial opinions

Ranking of destinations

Recommendations

Every article should remain factual regardless of commercial relationships.

---

# Mission

Partnerships should strengthen:

Education

Conservation

Research

Responsible travel

Regional development

Local communities

Cultural heritage

The objective is collaboration rather than advertising.

---

# Preferred Partners

Priority should be given to organizations that contribute positively to Southern Chile.

Examples include:

Museums

Universities

Research institutions

Conservation organizations

Protected areas

Regional governments

Municipalities

Responsible tourism operators

Local guides

Hotels with sustainable practices

Transport companies

Photographers

Publishers

Cultural organizations

---

# Commercial Operators

Commercial businesses may become partners if they demonstrate:

Professionalism

Legal compliance

Respect for nature

Respect for local communities

Responsible tourism practices

Transparency

Partnerships should never be based solely on financial contribution.

---

# Editorial Partners

Editorial partners may provide:

Interviews

Historical information

Photography

Scientific information

Regional expertise

Maps

Archival material

Editorial review

Their participation should always be acknowledged when appropriate.

---

# Sponsored Content

Sponsored content must always be clearly identified.

Readers should never confuse advertising with editorial content.

Transparency is mandatory.

---

# Affiliate Links

Future affiliate links are acceptable if:

They are clearly disclosed.

They are relevant.

They do not influence editorial recommendations.

The reader's trust comes first.

# Community Collaboration

The publication welcomes collaboration from:

Researchers

Historians

Photographers

Park rangers

Museum staff

Scientists

Local residents

Community organizations

---

# What We Will Not Accept

The publication will not knowingly collaborate with organizations that:

Damage protected areas

Misrepresent Indigenous cultures

Encourage irresponsible wildlife interactions

Provide misleading information

Promote unsafe activities

Pressure editorial decisions

Mislead readers

Editorial credibility is more valuable than short-term revenue.

---

# Long-Term Vision

End of the World Travel aims to become a trusted editorial platform connecting readers with the people, institutions and organizations that help preserve and understand Southern Chile.

Partnerships should strengthen knowledge, conservation and responsible travel—not replace the publication's independent editorial voice.

Trust is the project's most valuable asset.
