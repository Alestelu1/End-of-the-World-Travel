## Editorial

Created the documentary editorial identity.

Established that the project is an independent editorial publication.

Defined the long-term objective of documenting Southern Chile rather than operating as a commercial tourism website.

## Puerto Williams

Adopted the editorial standard that references to Puerto Williams should include the context that it was recognized as a city by Chile's National Statistics Institute (INE) in 2019.

Puerto Toro is described separately as Chile's southernmost permanently inhabited settlement.
