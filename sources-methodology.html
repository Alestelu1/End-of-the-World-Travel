Sources & Methodology

About This Project

About This Project

End of the World Travel is an independent project focused on southern Chile, Tierra del Fuego, Puerto Williams, Cape Horn, and Antarctica. The goal is to document places, routes, landscapes, and travel experiences while providing geographical and cultural context for visitors interested in the far south. This site is developed and maintained independently and continues to evolve as new destinations, photographs, and research are added.

Editorial Approach

Editorial Approach

The purpose of this project is to share information about southern Chile in a clear and accessible way. We aim to provide useful context about destinations, routes, geography, and travel experiences while avoiding exaggerated claims and promotional language. Content is reviewed and updated as new information becomes available.

Sources

Sources

Information on this site is based on maps, published research, local publications, travel information, and first-hand observations when available. We consult a variety of sources to better understand destinations, transportation, geography, and travel logistics across southern Chile and Antarctica. As new information becomes available, content may be updated and refined.

Photography & Media

Photography & Media

Photographs on this site are used to help illustrate places, landscapes, and travel experiences. Images may come from licensed sources, public collections, contributors, or original photography. When required, image credits and licensing information are provided separately. As the project grows, additional original photography and local contributions may be incorporated.
